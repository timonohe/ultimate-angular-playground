Angular Lifecycle

ngOnChanges
  • Called if any input member changed
  • Holds current and previous member value
  • Called before ngOnInit if component has any input members
 ngOnInit
  • Called first or after ngOnChanges
  • Method to initialize component members with complex code
 ngDoCheck
  • Checks for custom change detections
  • Called after each ngOnChanges and after ngOnInit
 ngAfterContentInit
  • Called after external content is loaded into that component
  • Called once after the first ngDoCheck
 ngAfterContentChecked
  • Called after external content that was projected checked by angular
  • Called after ngAfterContentInit and after every ngDoCheck
 ngAfterViewInit
  • Called after Angular initialized child views or displayed content
  • Called once after the first ngAfterContentChecked
 ngAfterViewChecked
  • Called after Angular checked the embedded or to displayed content
  • Called after ngAfterViewInit or every ngAfterContentChecked
 ngOnDestroy
  • Cleanup-Phase after Angular destoys the component
  • To avoid memory leaks unsubscribe here from every Observable
