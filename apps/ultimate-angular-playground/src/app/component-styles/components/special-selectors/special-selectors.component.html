:host
Every component can be embedded with their corresponding element-selector. The embedded element is also known as host-element. The selector for that element is :host. All styles will be applied to the host-element itself and to all of its descendants.
:host-context
If the style on the host-element should conditionally applied that depends on an ancestor of that element, a combination with the so called :host-context selector and a condition within the function brackets. :host-context(.active)
/deep/, >>>, and ::ng-deep (DEPRECATED)
The ng-deep-selector disables the View-Encapsulation from Angular in that specific block. Without putting a host-selector in front of this selector all styles within that block will be handled as global styles.
